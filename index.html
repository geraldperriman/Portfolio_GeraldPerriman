Check With Me

Check With Meclose

We all been victims of unwanted , random or spam phone calls at one time or another. Now with Check With Me you can search any United States domestic phone number to find out who is calling, their possible associaties and previous addresses, and where the call is coming from using the Google Maps API. With this great information you are then able to make a well informed decision on what numbers you should be blocking or not answering.

Check With Me is a web application that uses API calls to gather information from the Reverse Phone White Pages API and the Google Maps API. After the JSON object return from the WhitePages API, it then displays specific information to the user, then updates Google Maps with lat-long coordinates to show where the call is coming from.

Come Log Your Workout!

Swolely Grailclose

This is a web app that allows users to create custom workout routines and track their progress. Create an account, make a routine, and log your progress during each workout. Using the Meteor framework for NodeJS, the application can work both on your computer as well as mobile device. The ability to move seemlessly between computer to mobile, is what makes our app ideal for those looking to get in shape. Spare your thumbs by navigating to our webpage on your home computer to make a routine. And when you're ready to hit the gym, use the app to log your progress between each set.
